## Scoring rules

- `freq` and `pain` ∈ `high | med | low`; persona ∈ `pro-code | customizer | both`; target ∈ `on-prem | online | both`.
- **Research-noise mitigation:** `high` frequency requires confirmation from ≥2 independent source families. Rows backed by a single family are capped at `med` (even when the harvest row claimed `high`).
- **Merged rows:** pain = MAX across merged sources; frequency = consensus across families (ties resolved toward the better-justified signal). One consolidated one-clause justification per signal.
- **Target** is the task's target, not the tool's — pac-cli rows are marked by where the *task* exists; pac's online-only limitation stays in `tooling-today`. `on-prem→online` migration rows are recorded as `on-prem` (the org being migrated). Harvest target parentheticals were extracted into the `target` column and stripped from scenario text.
- **Sources:** `family:URL`, `; `-separated; identical URLs cited by multiple families are written `familyA+familyB:URL`. Prior-art rows cite local research docs as `prior-art:<filename>`. The family prefix records which harvest file contributed the URL — not the URL's publisher domain (e.g. an `xrmtoolbox:` entry may point at learn.microsoft.com because the XrmToolBox harvest row cited that page).
- **Audit marker:** a `(single family)` parenthetical in the `freq` cell marks rows capped by the noise-mitigation rule; rows without it met the ≥2-family threshold.
